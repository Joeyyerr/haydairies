Rise & Shine!

What’s better than an educational orientation by our very own in-house experienced goat farmer? After the orientation, grab the opportunity for an up close and personal session with their farmer. Feel free to ask him any questions and he will try his best to answer your queries / fulfill your inquisitive mind!


Here are the 3 educational tour packages we have for the learning journey:


Group Package 1

Morning Groups (9:00am / 9:30am / 10:00am ) Pre-School / Primary School
S$8.00 + GST = S$8.56 per person
•Viewing of Goat Milking Session & 20 minutes Orientation Briefing. Upon arrival, visitors will get to view how the goats are being milked while receiving an explanation of their farm history, introduction to their lovely goats, understand the milking process as well as the benefits of their goat milk. After the orientation, visitors can proceed to view the goat farm vicinity at their own pace
•200ml bottle of Fresh Goat Milk for each participant
•Souvenir (Hay Dairies cotton bag) for each participant
•Hay Dairies Activity Book (with stickers) for each participant
•For every 4 paying participants, 1 packet of Alfalfa Hay will be given to feed the goats
Duration: Visitors typically stay at the farm for 45 minutes to 1 hour Minimum group size of 10 people. A surcharge of S$32.10 (inclusive of GST) applies if your headcount falls below 20.

Group Package 3

Afternoon Groups (A group of 10 or more people visiting Hay Dairies at/after 12:00pm)
S$5.00 per person
•200ml bottle of Fresh Goat Milk for each participant
•For every 4 paying participants, 1 packet of Alfalfa Hay will be given to feed the goats
•Faster entry into the farm and goat barn (for feeding of goat) is allowed for groups with pre-booking
There will be no goat milking session and no orientation briefing in the afternoon. The group can view the goat farm vicinity at their own pace along the goat viewing trail. Please note that Hay Dairies does not allow groups without booking to enter the farm.
