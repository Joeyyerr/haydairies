ABOUT THEM : The Only Goat Farm Since 1988


With more than 20 years of establishment, Hay Dairies is the only goat farm in Singapore since 1988. Our farm has more than 800 goats of mixed breed with origin heritage from Minnesota, United States.

Free & Easy Tours

Families and individuals of a group less than 10 people can walk-in to the farm and enjoy a free & easy tour around our premises (prior booking is not required).

Educational Tours

For 10 or more people, we will consider you as a group and entrance fee will be chargeable with an educational farm tour package included. We offer 3 educational tour packages!.
